═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
                              INTERACTION FLOW
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

VISITOR FLOW
  1. Opens index.html
  2. Browses home page
  3. Fills "Get in Touch" form
  4. Submits form
  5. Sees success message
  6. Clicks product link (wood.html, chemicals.html, pharma.html)
  7. Views products
  8. Filters by category
  9. Requests quote
  10. Fills quote form
  11. Quote saved to localStorage
  12. Returns to home page

ADMIN FLOW
  1. Clicks "Login" button
  2. Enters demo credentials
  3. Redirected to dashboard
  4. Views analytics
  5. Sees recent leads
  6. Clicks "Products" tab
  7. Views product list
  8. Adds new product
  9. Edits existing product
  10. Deletes product
  11. Switches categories
  12. Logs out
  13. Redirected to login page
